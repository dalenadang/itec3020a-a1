Pho

Description:

Pho is a popular Vietnamese soup that is typically served as a flavorful noodle dish. It is considered one of Vietnam's national dishes and has gained international popularity.

Details:
  • Broth:Pho broth is usually made from simmering beef bones and sometimes with added spices, such as star anise, cloves, and cinnamon
  • Noodles:Pho is typically served with flat, rice noodles that are soft and chewy.
  • Garnishes:Pho is traditionally served with a variety of fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime wedges, sliced chili peppers, and hoisin and sriracha sauces.
